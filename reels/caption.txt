🕉️ Bhagavad Gita Wisdom

🌿 Right Action

📜 Sanskrit:
तस्मादसक्तः सततं कार्यं कर्म समाचर।

✨ English:
Perform your duty without attachment.
Let your actions become worship.

🌼 Hindi:
आसक्ति छोड़कर कर्म करो।
अपने कर्म को साधना बनाओ।

📌 Reference: Bhagavad Gita Bhagavad Gita 3.19
