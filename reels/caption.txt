🌿 Consistency

📜 Sanskrit:
यतो यतो निश्चरति मनश्चञ्चलमस्थिरम्।

✨ English:
Bring the restless mind back again and again.

🌼 Hindi:
भटकते मन को बार-बार वापस लाओ।

📌 Reference: Bhagavad Gita 6.26
